Navigate to Organization Members:

 

       Click your name on the right of the top menu bar

       Click Manage Organizations

       Click Organization Members on the left vertical menu.

 

 

 

Function

Description

Invite Someone (to your organization)

Use this option to invite someone that is already registered to NuvIoT

Register Someone (to your organization)

Use this option to invite someone who has not already registered to NuvIoT

Role

Options:

       Account disabled

       End User

       App Builder

       Admin

Note: Invites set the user to End User and you can only change that once they have accepted the invite and show up in the members section

Invitations

A list of outstanding invites which can be cancelled or resend.
